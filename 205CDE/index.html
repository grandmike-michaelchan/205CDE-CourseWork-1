How to play the Paper-Scissor-Rock game

Play Paper-Scissor-Rock game against computer

(CityU Scope ITB, March 2020)

You will be playing Paper-Scissor-Rock against the computer. Player needs to enter name to play. Once name is entered, Paper-Scissor- Rock images will appear and player will pick his/ her choice by clicking the one of the images.

The main screen has Paper-Scissor-Rck images changing in high speed. When the player pick the image from he right, the computer will randomly select one to compare against.

At the bottom part of the main section displays the score. The winner earns a point but no point is lost for tie or loser. If the player wants to play again, he/ she can click the "Play Again" button or just simply click the choice on one of the paper-scissor-rock images.

Have fun and enjoy!
